About

San Antonio native born and raised on the southside, Angie Riojas is a contributing member of the arts community exhibiting work in galleries, on stage and the runway. Most weekdays she is in the salon cutting and coloring hair, weekends are often booked up styling for weddings and events throughout the city and surrounding hill country. Art and culture are a major influence on her work which can be seen at different local events throughout the year.
